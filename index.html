Services

Consulting process begins with an initial meeting with clients to gather business goals, product requirements, and user experience. Once these requirements are defined, I provide an estimated cost and time to market.

Projects are built utilizing an iterative process - frequent communication with clients is a key part of the process. This allows for a short feedback loop in which clients can view progress as the project is built incrementally. Clients will have access to a staging environment while the product is in development. Once complete, it will be deployed and will be "live".
