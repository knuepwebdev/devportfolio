Services

Web development consultant for early-stage startups and individuals. Free consultations are provided.

The initial consultation is to gather business goals, product requirements, and user experience. Once these requirements are defined, an estimated cost and time to market will be provided.

Projects are developed utilizing an iterative process with frequent communication with clients. This allows for a short feedback loop in which clients can view demos as the project is built and deployed incrementally.
